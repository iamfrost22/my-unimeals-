Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of university students tired of the same old dining hall food, UniMeals began as a small campus initiative to provide better meal options for busy students. What started as a passion project in a dorm kitchen has grown into a thriving service supporting thousands of students across multiple campuses.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to fresh, healthy, and affordable meals without compromising on taste or convenience. Our mission is to revolutionize campus dining by delivering chef-prepared meals directly to students, saving them time and providing nourishment that fuels academic success.

Our Ingredients

We partner with local farms and suppliers within 100 miles to source the freshest ingredients possible. All our meats are hormone-free, our produce is seasonal, and we accommodate all major dietary restrictions including gluten-free, vegan, halal, and kosher options. We never use artificial preservatives or flavor enhancers.

Our Journey

2020

Founded in a Dorm Kitchen

Started with 3 students delivering homemade meals to friends. First month served 50 meals to fellow computer science students.

2021

University Partnership

Formalized agreement with campus dining services. Hired first professional chef and expanded to serve 500 students weekly.

2022

Commercial Kitchen

Moved operations to a professional campus kitchen facility. Launched mobile app and expanded menu to 30+ weekly options.

2023

Expanded to 5 Campuses

Now serving thousands of students daily across multiple universities. Recognized by "Campus Life" magazine as top food innovation.

Future

National Expansion

Plans to bring UniMeals to 25+ campuses by 2025 with sustainable packaging initiatives and student employment programs.
